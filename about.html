About Vermont Small Business Navigator

Vermont Small Business Navigator is a public resource created to help entrepreneurs, makers, and small business owners succeed in Vermont. Whether you're starting a business from scratch or looking to expand, we offer tools, information, and connections to get you there.

Our mission is to simplify the complex process of doing business in Vermont by offering centralized access to resources including:

  • Step-by-step business startup guides
  • Local and state funding opportunities
  • Regulatory compliance help
  • Training events and workshops
  • Support networks and community partnerships

Who We Are

This site is managed in collaboration with Vermont-based business advisors, economic development agencies, and entrepreneurs. Our content is informed by real business needs across the state—from Burlington to Brattleboro and everywhere in between.

Our Partners

  • Vermont Small Business Development Center (VtSBDC)
  • Vermont Economic Development Authority (VEDA)
  • Local Chambers of Commerce
  • Vermont Agency of Commerce and Community Development
  • Community-Based Nonprofits

Why We Built This

Small businesses are the backbone of Vermont’s economy, but many entrepreneurs struggle to find clear, up-to-date information on what’s required to start or run a business. This platform brings those answers together, in plain language, with links to the people and tools that can help you grow your business sustainably and successfully.
