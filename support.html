Local Business Support Across Vermont

Vermont has a rich network of organizations and advisors ready to support entrepreneurs, makers, farmers, and small business owners. From free one-on-one advising to incubator space, you're never far from expert help.

One-on-One Business Advising

  • Vermont Small Business Development Center (VtSBDC)
    Free business advising for startups and existing businesses across Vermont.
  • SCORE Vermont
    Free mentoring from experienced business professionals, available in-person and online.
  • Center for Women & Enterprise Vermont
    Business support for women, BIPOC, and underserved entrepreneurs.

Local Economic Development Organizations

  • Regional Development Corporations (RDCs) – Connect with your county-level economic development team.
  • Local Chambers of Commerce – Networking, events, and advocacy in your region.
  • Vermont Community Action Agencies – Support for low-income entrepreneurs and rural business owners.

Business Incubators & Co-Working Spaces

  • The Lightning Jar (Bennington) – Co-working and entrepreneur programming.
  • Hannaford Career Center (Middlebury) – Small business labs and training.
  • Center for an Agricultural Economy (Hardwick) – Food systems incubation and support.

Support for Specific Groups

  • Veteran-Owned Businesses – Access special mentoring, grants, and loan programs.
  • BIPOC Entrepreneurs – Connect with equity-focused business resources.
  • Youth & Student Entrepreneurs – Programs through schools, colleges, and nonprofits.
